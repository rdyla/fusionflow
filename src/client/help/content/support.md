## Support

Support shows your Packet Fusion service cases (telecom/CCaaS), pulled from our CRM.

- The dashboard highlights active, stale, and customer-blocked cases.
- Open a case to see its detail, notes, and status.

Note: this is for **service** cases. For help *using CloudConnect itself*, use the **?** button's **Still need help?** instead — it routes straight to a CloudConnect admin.
